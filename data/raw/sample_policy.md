# SecureHealth Insurance Policy

## Policy Overview

The SecureHealth Insurance Policy provides financial coverage for eligible
medical expenses incurred by the insured person during the policy period.

The policy is valid for one year from the start date mentioned in the policy
schedule.

## Eligibility

Applicants must be at least 18 years old.

Dependent children can be covered from the age of 90 days until they reach
25 years of age.

## Waiting Period

A waiting period of 30 days applies from the policy start date.

Medical expenses incurred during this waiting period are not covered unless
they result from an accident.

Pre-existing diseases are covered after a waiting period of 24 months.

## Hospitalization Coverage

The policy covers hospitalization expenses when the insured person is admitted
to a recognized hospital for at least 24 consecutive hours.

Covered expenses include:

- Room charges
- Doctor fees
- Nursing expenses
- Medication
- Diagnostic tests
- Surgical procedures

## Claim Documents

The insured person must submit:

- Completed claim form
- Hospital discharge summary
- Medical bills and receipts
- Doctor prescriptions
- Diagnostic reports
- Proof of identity

## Exclusions

The policy does not cover cosmetic surgery unless it is medically necessary
because of an accident.

Treatment resulting from alcohol or illegal drug abuse is excluded.

Non-prescribed experimental treatments are also excluded.

## Claim Notification

The insurer must be informed within 48 hours of an emergency hospitalization.

For planned hospitalization, the insurer must be informed at least 72 hours
before admission.
